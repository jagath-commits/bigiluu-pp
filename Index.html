Privacy Policy

Welcome to Bigiluu. This page explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data to reassure you that your privacy is respected and protected.

Data Protection

Bigiluu values your privacy. This privacy policy explains how your information is used, collected, and protected when you use our reading platform.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Username and account credentials
  • Profile image
  • Posts created by users and public activity
  • Reading activity and interactions
  • Saved posts and bookmarks

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the reading platform
  • Display user profiles and securely host users' posts
  • Track reader counts and generate insights
  • Enable sharing features and internal community growth

3. Content Visibility

Posts published on Bigiluu may be visible to other users of the platform depending on your interaction. We strive to create an engaging public reading environment while maintaining your core account privacy.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable and industry-standard security steps to protect user information and maintain platform security. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Bigiluu does not sell or share personal user data with third parties except when required by law or to protect our platform's legal rights and safety.

6. User Control

Users can actively control the content they publish and have the ability to manage or update their profile information at any time directly through the application.

7. Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect platform improvements, legal regulations, or other operational changes. Any updates will be applied immediately online.

8. Child Safety Standards

At Bigilu, we are committed to ensuring a safe environment for all users, especially children.

  • We do not allow any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE).
  • Users are strictly prohibited from sharing inappropriate or harmful content.
  • We provide a reporting system where users can report abusive behavior.
  • We take immediate action including account suspension or removal.
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws and regulations.
